# Use Cases — full content (verbatim, for the expanded #usecases section)

Organize by audience group. Each group = a header + a tight bullet list. Pro items marked "coming".
All non-Pro items are REAL, SHIPPED capabilities — make that clear.

## 🎬 Short-form / Creator
- Turn a 20-minute recording into a tight 60-second reel — auto-removes filler words, dead air, and false starts
- Cut a podcast into viral clips — finds the hook, trims to the payoff
- Add word-synced karaoke captions — every word highlighted on the syllable, not near it
- Cut on the beat — automatically syncs cuts and animations to the music's BPM
- Reframe landscape → 9:16 vertical for Reels, Shorts, TikTok
- Punch-in zooms on key moments — emphasis where it matters
- Generate a viral-style reel from raw footage with one prompt
- Platform-specific delivery — export tuned for Reels / Shorts / TikTok / YouTube / feed

## 📊 SaaS / Product / Startup
- Make a product demo video without an editor — show your app in browser chrome with arrows, highlight boxes, spotlight-dim
- Launch films — cinematic intros with logo reveals and specular sweeps
- Animated data-stories — bar/line/area charts that grow, numbers that count up
- Feature walkthroughs — annotated screen recordings
- Sales & pitch videos
- Investor update videos
- Changelog / release videos
- Onboarding & tutorial videos
- Ad creative — hook → beats → CTA structure

## ✂️ Editing (the classic NLE)
- Multi-track timeline (video, audio, image, text, Lottie)
- Trim, split, ripple delete, drag, snap, razor
- Speed / retime, keyframe animation with easing lanes
- Transforms, crop, opacity, blend modes
- Transitions (cross-dissolve, wipe, push, glitch, RGB-split)
- Layouts — picture-in-picture, split-screen, grid
- Color grading — exposure, contrast, saturation, temperature, palette-matched grades
- Audio — waveforms, volume, fades, mixing
- Export — H.264 / H.265 / ProRes, 1080p or 4K

## 🧠 AI-Operated (the differentiator)
- "Cut the boring parts" — silence and pause detection
- "Remove every 'um'" — word-level surgical cutting
- "Caption this" — word-accurate, styled, animated
- "Make this a viral reel" — full workflow: reframe → hook → cut → caption → grade
- "Pull my brand colors from this clip" — palette extraction → auto-grade
- "Animate an intro that says [X]" — bespoke motion graphics from a sentence
- "Find the best moment" — it watches your frames and picks
- Claude designs the whole film — art-directed compositions, not templates
